## About the App
Flashcards Swipe helps you study and memorize easily with responsive flashcards.  
Create your own sets, swipe to review, and choose from multiple visual themes.

---

## FAQ
- **How do I create a flashcard set?**  
  Go to *Create*, add cards, save, and start practicing.

- **How do I practice?**  
  Open any flashcard set you’ve created. You’ll see two options:  
  • **View** – to simply browse through the cards.  
  • **Practice** – to review with swipe gestures.  
  In practice mode, swipe **right** if you remember the card, or **left** if you don’t.

- **Can I use pre-made sets?**  
  Yes — Romanian, English, and German sets are included.  
  To access them, go to the **"Some collections"** page, where you can practice:  
  • English → Romanian  
  • Romanian → English  
  • German → Romanian  

  These sets can also be added to your personal list of created sets, so you can review them alongside your own custom flashcards.


- **How do I change text size or theme?**  
  To adjust the **text size**, go to *Account > Settings*.  
  To change the **visual theme**, go to *Account > My Theme*.  
  This way you can personalize the app’s appearance to match your preferences.
